Are you tired of listening to the same music?

Do you need some music suggestions?

You've come to the right page. Here on Music Rater, you can rate any music you would like.

Hello, this is a Music rating App that is designed to search for your favorite music and rate them.

If your favorite music is not there, you can easily add it to our database and feel free to rate it.

Make sure to include the Title, Artist, and Year when adding a new submission.

Do you think you have to pay for this service? If you said "Yes", think again.

Music Rater is completely free to use. You do not have to pay for anything.

But, if you are feeling like giving something, feel free to donate. Just fill out the Contact Us form.
